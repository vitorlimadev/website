Owning a place on the internet

Posted at 2021-08-12



Recently I’ve watched Luke Smith’s video where he talks about his project landchad.net, a website with information on how to setup multiple services yourself, and stop relying on external services for everything you do on the internet.

I don’t use any social media (besides an account on LinkedIn), but I didn’t have a place to actually speak whatever I want without the all-watching eyes of big-tech. This is why I made this blog. To write whatever I want, and express my opinions freely.

And maybe this might inspire you to do the same, to try building your actual space on the internet. Because if everything you have is inside servers from other companies/people, your digital presence relies on their will to maintain and deliver your data.

I will probably write a lot about technology in here, since this is what I’m thinking about most of the time. I hope you enjoy it!
